Welcome To Atithi Advisor

Every coin has two sides and so does every story have one other perception..!!

We at ATITHIADVISOR are providing a platform to share this other side of the story. ATITHIADVISOR is primarily for all Stay Facility Providers in hospitality industry like Hotels, Homestays, Campsites, Bagpackers’ Stays and unique experience stays such as Treehouses, Igloos, Houseboats etc. Facility Providers will now have opportunity to share their experience with the Atithi that have stayed with them and used their facilities on a common platform. Registered Users of ATITHIADVISOR can even search feedback of a guest shared by other Registered Facility Provider. Getting these insights will help the Facility Provider to “know the Customer” beforehand and this preparedness will assist in better service to the Guest.

Guest also has an option to log into ATITHIADVISOR platform and find out their ratings submitted by Facility Providers where they have stayed. Once Guest is a “Super Guest”...host of benefits awaits the other side...!!!
